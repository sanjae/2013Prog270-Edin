MongoLab

Fully managed cloud database service with many great features like:

  • highly available MongoDB databases
  • automatic backups
  • web-based tools
  • monitoring and support service

Products

MongoLab products fall in two tiers of plans: Shared and Dedicated. Dedicated service is a little more expensive, but includes service like: full database admin privileges and ability to firewall, 24x7 emergency support email and commercial support with one hour SLA.

MongoLab is powered by MongoDB

MongoDB is an open-source database and the leader in NoSQL database. It offers features like: document-oriented storage, Full index support, replication and high availability just to name a few.
