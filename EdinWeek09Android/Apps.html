Flipboard and Zinio

I was able to find Flipboard in the Play Store. Here is what "ThinkDigit" had to say about the app:

Available for iOS for over a year now, Flipboard is your
          personalized magazine app for not only consuming news content but also keeping track of your YouTube 
          subscriptions and social networking feeds...Navigating through the app is as simple as flipping through the 
          stories one at a time. Users can flip up/down to see the next/previous story on the list. 
          You can go through the stories either through your predefined feeds such as Google Reader or the 
          preselected categories.While reading the story, you have the option to tweet it, 
          add the story to your favourite list and share the link. 
          You also have the option to view the story on the original website on the webpage.
